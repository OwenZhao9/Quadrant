Quadrant Support

Task management based on the Eisenhower Matrix

About Quadrant

Quadrant helps you organize tasks by urgency and importance using four quadrants. All data is stored locally on your device — no account required, no data collected.

Features

  • Four-quadrant task management (Eisenhower Matrix)
  • Task completion history with calendar view
  • Chinese and English language support
  • Dark mode support
  • 100% local data storage, protecting your privacy

FAQ

Will I lose my data after updating the app?
No. All your task data is stored locally and will be preserved after app updates.

Does Quadrant collect any personal data?
No. Quadrant does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.
