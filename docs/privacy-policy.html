Privacy Policy for Quadrant

Effective date: September 3, 2025

Quadrant is a local task management app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how that information is used.

Information We Collect

Quadrant does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location through the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores task content, completion history, language preferences, and theme preferences locally on your device so the app can function properly.

Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Quadrant does not use third-party analytics, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not display third-party advertising.

Data Sharing

Quadrant does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Your task data remains on your device unless you choose to share your device data through your own backup or system services.

Children's Privacy

Quadrant does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact: zhaoningup@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
